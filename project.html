Project 1: Tech Education Website

I came up with a web page where learners who are ambitious of learning tech courses can be able to apply, learn and receive certificates after completion.

The website contains different pages like:

  • Login Page: where learners can log into the website after logging out.
  • Registration Page: where new learners can create accounts and access available courses.
  • Course Catalog Page: where learners can see all available courses and their durations.
  • Course-Details Page: detailed description of each course and its contents.
  • Certification Page: where learners can view and download awarded certificates.
  • Feedback Page: where learners can provide feedback about the courses and the website.

This ensures easy navigation and access to every page on the website.

Project 2: Tech Job Portal

Due to most people in tech struggling to get tech jobs, I created a website where companies, tech-employed professionals, and job seekers can interact and apply for jobs.

The website contains different pages like:

  • Registration Page: where new users can create accounts and access jobs, mentors, and professionals.
  • Login Page: where mentors, job seekers, professionals, and collaborators can log back into their accounts.
  • Job Listing Page: where tech jobs are posted with descriptions, company details, and application steps.
  • Mentorship Page: connects learners with experienced professionals for advice and career support.
  • User Profile: where users can showcase their skills, experience, and achievements.
  • Job Application Page: where job seekers can apply directly to job posts.
  • Community Forum/Chat Page: members can discuss, ask questions, share knowledge, and chat directly with mentors and other job seekers.
  • Job Category Page: jobs are categorized and displayed based on their types.
  • Collaboration Page: users can collaborate on tech projects, share ideas, and join projects seeking collaborators.
